About the Project

VisaCheck is a mobile application designed to simplify international travel. It provides users with up-to-date visa information and a curated "Traveler Toolkit" to book essential services immediately.

Integrated Partners: Trip.com (Flights & Stays), Ekta Insurance (Visa-compliant coverage).
